# Volleyball Rules

SECTION 3 – DIAGRAMS

DIAGRAM 1a: COMPETITION / CONTROL AREA

Relevant rules: 1, 1.4.5, 4.2.1, 4.2.3.1, 15.4.2, 21.3.2.1, 21.3.3.1, 27.1, 28.1.

The diagram illustrates, for FIVB, world and official competitions:

- the competition/control area around the court;
- the position and approximate size of the team benches (about 3 x 3 m);
- the scorer’s table and the Technical Delegate’s (TD) table;
- the Libero replacement zone and the substitution zone;
- locations of LED panels and minimum safety distances (for example 20 cm, 15 cm, 5 cm, 1 m, 1.75 m, etc.);
- warm‑up areas (options 1 and 2) and the minimum width of the free zone (at least 3 m, recommended up to 6.5 m);
- overall dimensions of the free zone (minimum 24–31 m in length).

The values shown apply to FIVB, world and official competitions.

DIAGRAM 1b: THE PLAYING AREA

Relevant rules: 1, 1.4, 1.4.2, 1.4.3, 1.4.4, 1.4.5, 4.2.1, 4.2.3.1, 15.10.1, 19.3.1.4, 19.3.2.7, 23.1, 24.1, 27.1, 28.1.

The diagram shows:

- the dimensions of the court (18 x 9 m);
- the front and back zones and the service zones (at least 3 m, recommended 5 m behind each end line);
- the minimum width of the free zone (at least 3 m, recommended 6.5 m) on all sides of the court;
- the height and location of the referee’s stand (eye level about 1.75 m above the net);
- typical distances from court lines to barriers and advertising boards.

These values also apply to FIVB, world and official competitions.
