4.5 FORBIDDEN OBJECTS

4.5.1 It is forbidden to wear objects that may cause injury or give a player
an artificial advantage.

4.5.2 The wearing of glasses or contact lenses is at the playerâ€™s own risk.

4.5.3 Compression pads (padded protective devices) may be worn for
protection or support.

For FIVB, world and official senior competitions, these devices must be
the same colour as the corresponding part of the uniform. Black,
white or neutral colours may also be used, provided that all players
wear the same colour.

5 TEAM LEADERSHIP

Both the team captain and the coach are responsible for the behaviour
and discipline of their team members.

Liberos may be either team captains or game captains.

5.1 CAPTAIN

5.1.1 Before the match, the team captain represents his team in the coin
toss and then signs the score sheet.

5.1.2 During the match and while on the court, the team captain is the
game captain. If the team captain is not on the court, the coach or the
team captain must designate another player on the court
