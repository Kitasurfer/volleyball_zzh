28 ASSISTANT SCORER

28.1 LOCATION

The assistant scorer carries out his/her function next to the scorer at the scorer’s table.

For FIVB, world and official competitions, an assistant scorer is required.

28.2 RESPONSIBILITIES

He/she records the substitutions involving the Libero.

He/she assists with the administrative tasks of the scorer.

If the scorer becomes unable to continue his/her work, he/she is replaced by the assistant scorer.
