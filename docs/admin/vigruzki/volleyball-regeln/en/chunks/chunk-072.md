23 FIRST REFEREE

23.1 LOCATION

The first referee performs his/her function from a referee’s stand located at one end of the net on the opposite side from the scorer. His/her eye level must be approximately 50 cm above the top of the net.

23.2 AUTHORITY

23.2.1 The first referee directs the match from start to finish. He/she has authority over all members of the refereeing team and the team members. During the match, his/her decisions are final. He/she is authorized to overrule the decisions of other members of the refereeing team.
