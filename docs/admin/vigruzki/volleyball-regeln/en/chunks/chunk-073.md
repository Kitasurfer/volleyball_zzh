23.2.2 He/she also supervises the work of the ball retrievers and floor wipers.

23.2.3 He/she is authorized to decide on all matters related to the match, including those not expressly provided for in the Rules.

23.2.4 He/she must not allow any discussion of his/her decisions.

At the request of the game captain, however, the first referee gives an explanation of the application or interpretation of the Rules on which his/her decision was based.

If the game captain is not satisfied with this explanation and wishes to protest the decision, he/she must reserve the right to file this protest and have it recorded immediately after the end of the match. The first referee must acknowledge this right of the game captain.

23.2.5 The first referee is responsible, before and during the match, for determining whether the court equipment and conditions meet the requirements for the match.
