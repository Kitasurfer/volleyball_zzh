25.2 DUTIES

24.3.1 At the beginning of each set, at the change of courts in the deciding set and whenever necessary, the second referee performs the checks.

24.3.2 During the match, he/she decides, whistles and signals:

24.3.2.1 penetration into the opponent’s court and the space under the net;

24.3.2.2 positional faults of the receiving team;

24.3.2.3 illegal contact of a player with the net, mainly (but not exclusively) on the blocker’s side, and with the antenna on his/her side of the court;

24.3.2.4 a completed block by back‑row players or an attempted block by the Libero, or an attack‑hit fault by back‑row players or by the Libero;

24.3.2.5 the ball contacting an external object;

24.3.2.6 the ball contacting the floor when the first referee is not in a position to see the contact;
