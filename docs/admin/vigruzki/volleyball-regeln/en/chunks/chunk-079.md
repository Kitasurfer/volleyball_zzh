# Volleyball Rules

The scorer works in cooperation with the referees.
He/she uses a buzzer or other audible device to report irregularities or to give signals to the referees within his/her responsibilities.

27.2.1 Before the match and each set, the scorer:

27.2.1.1 records the data of the match and the teams, including the names and numbers of the Liberos, in accordance with the applicable procedures, and obtains the signatures of the game captains and coaches;

27.2.1.2 records the starting line‑up of each team from the line‑up sheet (or checks the data transmitted electronically).

If he/she does not receive the line‑up sheets on time, he/she immediately informs the second referee.

27.2.2 During the match, the scorer:

27.2.2.1 records the points scored;

27.2.2.2 controls the order of service of each team and notifies the referees of any fault immediately after the service;

27.2.2.3 is authorized to confirm and announce substitution requests with the buzzer, control the number of players, record substitutions and time‑outs, and inform the second referee;

27.2.2.4 informs the referees of any request for a regular game interruption that is not correct;

27.2.2.5 informs the referees of the end of sets and of the scoring of the 8th point in the deciding set;

27.2.2.6 records warnings for misconduct, sanctions and illegal requests;
