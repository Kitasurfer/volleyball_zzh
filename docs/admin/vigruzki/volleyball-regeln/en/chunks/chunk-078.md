26.2 RESPONSIBILITIES

The reserve referee is required to:

26.2.1 wear an official refereeing uniform while performing his/her duties;

26.2.2 replace the second referee in case of his/her absence, inability to continue, or when the second referee becomes the first referee;

26.2.3 before the match and between sets, check the substitution paddles (if they are used);

26.2.4 check the operation of the team bench tablets before and between sets, if a problem arises;

26.2.5 assist the second referee in supervising the substitution zone;

26.2.6 help the second referee ensure that an expelled/disqualified team member leaves the team dressing room;

26.2.7 monitor the substitute players in the warm‑up area and on the bench;

26.2.8 immediately after the player introductions, bring four match balls to the second referee and, after the checking of the players, give one match ball to the second referee;

26.2.9 assist the first referee in supervising the work of the floor wipers.

27 SCORER

27.1 LOCATION

The scorer sits at the scorer’s table on the side of the court opposite the first referee and facing the court.

27.2 RESPONSIBILITIES

The scorer fills in the score sheet in accordance with the Rules, working in cooperation with the second referee.
