10.2 BALL TOUCHES THE NET

When crossing the net, the ball may touch it.

10.3 BALL IN THE NET

10.3.1 A ball driven into the net may be recovered within the limits of the
team’s three hits.

10.3.2 If the ball tears the mesh of the net or causes it to break or becomes
caught in it, the rally is cancelled and replayed.

11 PLAYERS AT THE NET

11.1 CROSSING BEYOND THE NET

11.1.1 When blocking, a player may touch the ball beyond the net, provided
that he/she does not interfere with the opponent’s play before or
during the opponent’s attack hit.

11.1.2 After an attack hit, a player is permitted to pass his/her hand beyond
the net, provided that the initial contact with the ball was made within
his/her own playing space and the ball was not caught or thrown.

11.2 PENETRATION UNDER THE NET

11.2.1 Penetration into the opponent’s space under the net is permitted,
provided that it does not interfere with the opponent’s play.

11.2.2 Penetration into the opponent’s court beyond the centre line:

11.2.2.1 touching the opponent’s court with a foot (feet) is permitted,
provided that some part of the penetrating foot (feet) remains in
contact with or directly above the centre line, and this action does not
interfere with the opponent’s play.
