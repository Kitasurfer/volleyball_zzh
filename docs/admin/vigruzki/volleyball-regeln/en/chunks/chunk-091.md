# Volleyball Rules

DIAGRAM 11: OFFICIAL HAND SIGNALS OF THE REFEREES (PART 4)

24. **Ball touched**  
Brush the palm of one hand with the fingers of the other hand held vertically.

25. **Delay warning / delay penalty**  
Cover the wrist with a yellow card (warning) and with a red card (penalty).

DIAGRAM 12: OFFICIAL FLAG SIGNALS OF THE LINE JUDGES

Legend: **L** – line judge.

1. **Ball "in"**  
Point the flag downwards.

2. **Ball "out"**  
Raise the flag vertically.

3. **Ball touched**  
Raise the flag and, with the free hand, touch the tip of the flag.

4. **Fault in crossing space / ball touching an external object / foot fault of any player at the moment of service**  
Wave the flag above the head and then point to the antenna or to the relevant line.

5. **Impossible judgement**  
Raise and cross both arms and hands in front of the chest.
