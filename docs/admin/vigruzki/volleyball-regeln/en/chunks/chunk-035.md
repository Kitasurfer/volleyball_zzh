7 STRUCTURE OF PLAY

7.1 THE TOSS

Before the match, the first referee carries out a toss to determine the
first service and the sides of the court for the first set.

If a deciding set is to be played, a new toss is carried out.

7.1.1 The toss is conducted in the presence of both team captains.

7.1.2 The winner of the toss chooses:

EITHER

7.1.2.1 the right to serve or receive the service,

OR

7.1.2.2 the side of the court.

The loser takes the remaining choice.

7.2 OFFICIAL WARM‑UP

7.2.1 Before the match, if the teams have previously had a court
exclusively at their disposal, they are entitled to a 6‑minute official
joint warm‑up at the net; otherwise they may have 10 minutes.

For FIVB, world and official competitions, the teams are entitled to a
10‑minute joint warm‑up at the net.
