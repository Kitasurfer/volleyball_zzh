4.1.3 Only the players entered on the score sheet are allowed to enter the

4.2 TEAM AREA

4.2.1 Players who are not in play should either sit on their team bench or
stay in their warm‑up area. The coach and the other team members sit
on the bench, but may leave it temporarily.

The team benches are located next to the scorer’s table, outside the
free zone.

4.2.2 Only members of the team line‑up are allowed to enter the playing
area, sit on the bench during the match, and take part in the official
warm‑up.

4.2.3 Players who are not in play may warm up without balls as follows:
4.2.3.1 during the match: in the warm‑up areas;
4.2.3.2 during time‑outs: in the free zone behind their court.

4.2.4 During the official intervals, players may warm up with balls within
their own free zone.

4.3 EQUIPMENT

A player’s equipment consists of a jersey, shorts, socks (the uniform)
and sports shoes.

4.3.1 The colour and design of the jerseys, shorts and socks must be
uniform for the whole team (except for the Libero). Jerseys must be
clean.
