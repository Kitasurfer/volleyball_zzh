# Volleyball Rules

1.4.2 Service zone
The service zone is a 9 m wide area behind each end line.

It is laterally limited by two short lines, each 15 cm long, drawn
20 cm behind the end line as extensions of the sidelines. The two
short lines are included in the width of the service zone.

In depth, the service zone extends to the end of the free zone.

1.4.3 Substitution zone
The substitution zone is limited by the extensions of the two attack
lines up to the scorer’s table.

1.4.4 Libero replacement zone
The Libero replacement zone is part of the free zone on the side of the
team benches, limited by the extension of the attack line to the end
line.

1.4.5 Warm-up area
For FIVB, world and official competitions, the warm-up areas,
measuring approximately 3 x 3 m, are located in the two bench corners
outside the free zone, where they do not obstruct the spectators’ view,
or alternatively behind the team bench where the stands start more
than 2.5 m from the playing surface.

1.5 TEMPERATURE
The minimum temperature may not be lower than 10° C (50° F).

For FIVB, world and official competitions, the maximum temperature is
set for each match by the FIVB Technical Delegate.

1.6 LIGHTING
The lighting should not be less than 300 lux.
