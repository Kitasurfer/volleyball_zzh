# Volleyball Rules

8 STATES OF PLAY

CHAPTER 4
PLAYING ACTIONS

8.1 BALL IN PLAY

The ball is in play from the moment of the service hit, after the first
refereeâ€™s whistle, until the ball is out of play.

8.2 BALL OUT OF PLAY

The ball is out of play at the moment of the fault whistled by one of the
referees; if no fault is committed, it is out of play at the moment of the
whistle.

8.3 BALL "IN"

The ball is "in" when any part of the ball touches the court, including the
boundary lines, at any time of its contact with the floor.

8.4 BALL "OUT"

The ball is "out" when:

8.4.1 all parts of the ball which touch the floor are completely outside the
boundary lines;

8.4.2 it touches an object outside the court, the ceiling or a person not in
play;

8.4.3 it touches the antennas, cables, posts or the net itself outside the side
bands;

8.4.4 it crosses the vertical plane of the net, either partially or completely,
outside the crossing space, except in the case of Rule 10.1.2;

8.4.5 it passes completely under the net through the lower space.
