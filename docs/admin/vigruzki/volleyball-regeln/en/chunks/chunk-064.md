19.3.2.5 A Libero replacement made after the whistle for service but before the
service hit should not be refused; however, at the end of the rally the game
captain must be informed that this is not a legal procedure and that a
repetition will be sanctioned as a delay.

19.3.2.6 Any further late Libero replacements lead to an immediate interruption of
the game and the imposition of a delay penalty. Which team serves next is
determined by the nature of the delay penalty.

19.3.2.7 The Libero and the player being replaced may enter or leave the court only
through the Libero replacement zone.

19.3.2.8 Libero replacements must be recorded on the Libero control sheet (if
used) or on the electronic score sheet.
