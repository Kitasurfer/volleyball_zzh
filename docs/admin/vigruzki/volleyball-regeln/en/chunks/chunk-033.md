# Volleyball Rules

5.3 ASSISTANT COACH

5.3.1 The assistant coach sits on the team bench but has no right to
interfere in the match.

5.3.2 If the coach must leave the team for any reason, including a sanction,
except for entering the court as a player, an assistant coach may
assume the coach’s duties for the duration of the coach’s absence,
after the game captain has informed the referees of this.

6.1.1 Point

A team scores a point:

6.1.1.1 by successfully grounding the ball on the opponent’s court;

6.1.1.2 when the opponent commits a fault;

6.1.1.3 when the opponent receives a penalty.

rules (or otherwise violates the rules). The referees judge the faults and
award the consequences in accordance with the rules:
