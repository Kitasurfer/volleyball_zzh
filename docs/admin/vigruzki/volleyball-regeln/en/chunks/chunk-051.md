# Volleyball Rules

13.2 RESTRICTIONS OF THE ATTACK HIT

13.2.1 A front‑row player may perform an attack hit at any height, provided
that the contact with the ball has been made within his/her own
playing space (except Rules 13.2.4 and 13.3.6).

13.2.2 A back‑row player may perform an attack hit at any height from
behind the front zone:

13.2.2.1 the player’s foot (feet) must neither touch nor cross the attack line at
take‑off;

13.2.2.2 the player may land in the front zone after the hit.

13.2.3 A back‑row player may also perform an attack hit from the front zone
if, at the moment of contact, part of the ball is lower than the top of
the net.

13.2.4 No player may complete an attack hit on the opponent’s service when
the ball is in the front zone and entirely above the top of the net.

13.3 FAULTS OF THE ATTACK HIT

13.3.1 A player hits the ball into the opponent’s court outside the boundaries.

13.3.2 A player hits the ball "out".

13.3.3 A back‑row player performs an attack hit from the front zone when
the ball is entirely above the top of the net at the moment of the hit.

13.3.4 A player performs an attack hit on the opponent’s service when the
ball is in the front zone and entirely above the top of the net.
