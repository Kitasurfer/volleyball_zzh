15.7 EXCEPTIONAL SUBSTITUTION

A player (other than a Libero) who cannot continue playing because of
injury/illness or expulsion/disqualification must be legally substituted.
If this is not possible, the team is entitled to make an EXCEPTIONAL
substitution, which goes beyond the limits of Rule 15.6.

An exceptional substitution means that any player who is not on the
court at the moment of the injury/illness/expulsion/disqualification,
except for the Libero, the second Libero or the regular substitute for
that player, may be substituted for the
injured/ill/expelled/disqualified player. The player who has been

substitution, but it must be recorded on the score sheet as part of the

15.8 SUBSTITUTE FOR EXPULSION OR DISQUALIFICATION

An expelled or disqualified player must be immediately replaced by a
legal substitution.
