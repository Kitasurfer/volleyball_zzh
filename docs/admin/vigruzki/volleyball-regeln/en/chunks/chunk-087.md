# Volleyball Rules

Diagram 9a: misconduct warnings and sanctions

The table summarises categories of misconduct, number of occurrences by the same team member, the sanction, card(s) shown and consequences.

**Minor misconduct**

- **Level 1:** no card, no sanction.
- **Level 2:** any team member may receive a **yellow card** as a formal warning. This is **not** a sanction, only prevention. Any later repetition of misconduct by the same member is sanctioned as below (rude/offensive/aggression).

**Rude conduct**

- **First occurrence (any team member):** **penalty**, red card. Consequence: one point and service to the opponents.
- **Second occurrence (same team member):** **expulsion**, red + yellow cards jointly. The team member must go to the team dressing room for the rest of the set.
- **Third occurrence (same team member):** **disqualification**, red + yellow cards separately. The team member must go to the team dressing room for the rest of the match.

**Offensive conduct**

- **First occurrence (any team member):** **expulsion**, red + yellow cards jointly. The team member must go to the team dressing room for the rest of the set.
- **Second occurrence (same team member):** **disqualification**, red + yellow cards separately. The team member must go to the team dressing room for the rest of the match.

**Aggression**

- **First occurrence (any team member):** **disqualification**, red + yellow cards separately. The team member must go to the team dressing room for the rest of the match.
