11.3 CONTACT WITH THE NET

11.3.1 Contact with the net by a player between the antennas, while
playing the ball, is a fault.

Playing the ball includes (among other actions) take‑off, the hit (or
attempt) and the landing, preparing for a new action.

11.3.2 Players may touch the posts, cables or any other object outside the
antennas, including the net itself, provided that this does not interfere
with play (exception: Rule 9.1.3).

11.3.3 When the ball is driven into the net and causes it to touch an
opponent, no fault is committed.

11.4 PLAYER FAULTS AT THE NET

11.4.1 A player touches the ball or an opponent in the opponent’s space
before or during the opponent’s attack hit.

11.4.2 A player interferes with the opponent’s play by penetrating into the
opponent’s space under the net.
