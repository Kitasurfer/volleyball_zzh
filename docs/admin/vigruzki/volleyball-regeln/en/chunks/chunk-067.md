# Volleyball Rules

19.5 SUMMARY

If the Libero is expelled or disqualified, he/she may be immediately replaced by the team’s second Libero. If the team has only one Libero, it has the right to re‑designate a new Libero.

20 REQUIREMENTS OF CONDUCT

CHAPTER 7
BEHAVIOUR OF PARTICIPANTS

20.1 SPORTSMANLIKE CONDUCT

20.1.1 Participants must know and abide by the "Official Volleyball Rules".

20.1.2 Participants must accept the referees’ decisions with sportsmanlike conduct, without disputing them.

In case of doubt, clarification may only be sought through the game captain.

20.1.3 Participants must refrain from any actions or behaviour intended to influence the referees’ decisions or to conceal faults committed by their team.

20.2 FAIR PLAY

20.2.1 Participants must behave respectfully and courteously in the spirit of FAIR PLAY, not only towards the referees but also towards other officials, opponents, teammates and spectators.

20.2.2 Communication between team members during the match is permitted.
