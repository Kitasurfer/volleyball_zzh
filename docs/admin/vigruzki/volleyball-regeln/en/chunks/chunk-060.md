17.1.1 If a serious accident occurs during the match, the referee must immediately stop play and allow medical assistance onto the court.

The rally is then replayed.

17.1.2 If an injured/ill player cannot be legally or exceptionally substituted, the player is given a recovery time of 3 minutes, but not more than once for the same player in the match.

If the player does not recover, the team is declared incomplete.

If external interference occurs during the match, play must be interrupted and the rally is replayed.
