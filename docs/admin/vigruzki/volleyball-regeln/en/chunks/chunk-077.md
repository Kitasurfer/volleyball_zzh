For FIVB, world and official competitions where the Video Challenge System is used, a challenge referee is mandatory.

The challenge referee performs his/her function in the challenge booth, located at a place designated by the FIVB Technical Delegate.

25.2.1 He/she oversees the challenge process and ensures that it is conducted in accordance with the applicable challenge regulations.

25.2.2 The challenge referee must wear an official refereeing uniform while performing his/her duties.

25.2.3 After the challenge, he/she informs the first referee about the nature of the fault.

25.2.4 At the end of the match, he/she signs the score sheet.

For FIVB, world and official competitions, a reserve referee is mandatory.
