7.3 TEAM STARTING LINE‑UP

7.3.1 There must always be six players per team in play.

The team starting line‑up indicates the order in which the players stand
on the court. This order must be maintained throughout the set.

7.3.2 Before the start of each set, the coach must indicate his team’s
starting line‑up on a line‑up sheet or via an electronic device, if used.
The properly completed and signed sheet is submitted to the second
referee or the scorer, or is sent electronically directly to the e‑scorer.

7.3.3 Players not included in a set’s starting line‑up are the substitutes for
that set (except the Liberos).

7.3.4 Once the line‑up sheet has been delivered to the second referee or the
scorer, no change in the line‑up may be authorized without a regular
substitution.
