# Volleyball Rules

15.4 TIME‑OUTS

15.4.1 Time‑outs must be requested with the official hand signal when the ball
is out of play and before the whistle for the service. All requested time‑outs
last 30 seconds.

For FIVB, world and official competitions, the length of time‑outs may be
adjusted if the FIVB approves this at the organizer’s request.

In FIVB, world and official competitions, it is mandatory to use the buzzer
and then the hand signal to request a time‑out.

15.4.2 During all time‑outs, players must go to the free zone near their own
team bench.

15.5 SUBSTITUTION

15.5.1 A substitution is the act by which a player, other than the Libero or his/her
replacement, enters the match, after being recorded by the scorer, to
occupy the position of another player who must leave the court at that
moment.

15.5.2 If the substitution is forced by an injury to a player during play, the
coach (or game captain) may give the corresponding hand signal.

15.6 LIMITATION OF SUBSTITUTIONS

15.6.1 A player in the starting line‑up may leave the court but only once in a
set, and may re‑enter but only once in that set, and only in his/her
previous position in the line‑up.
