15.10.4 When a team intends to make more than one substitution at the same time, all substitute players must enter the substitution zone simultaneously and be included in the same request. In this case, the substitutions must be carried out successively, one pair of players at a time. If one substitution is illegal, the legal one(s) is/are granted and the illegal one(s) is/are refused and subject to a delay sanction.
