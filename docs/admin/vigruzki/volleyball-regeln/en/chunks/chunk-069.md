# Volleyball Rules

21.3.1 Penalty

The first rude conduct by a team member in the match is sanctioned with a point and service for the opponent.

21.3.2 Expulsion

21.3.2.1 A team member penalized with expulsion must be legally/exceptionally substituted immediately if on the court and may not take part in the rest of the set and must go to the team dressing room, without further consequences for the match.

An expelled coach loses the right to intervene in the set and must go to the team dressing room until the end of the current set.

21.3.2.2 The first offensive conduct by a team member is sanctioned with expulsion without further consequences.

21.3.2.3 The second rude conduct by the same team member in the same match is sanctioned with expulsion without further consequences.

21.3.3 Disqualification

21.3.3.1 A team member penalized with disqualification must, if on the court, be legally/exceptionally substituted immediately and must go to the team dressing room for the rest of the match, without further consequences.

21.3.3.2 The first physical attack, or behaviour implying or threatening aggression, is sanctioned with disqualification without further consequences.
