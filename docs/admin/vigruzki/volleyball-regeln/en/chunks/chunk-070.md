# Volleyball Rules

21.3.3.4 The third rude conduct by the same team member in the same match is sanctioned with disqualification without further consequences.

21.4 APPLICATION OF SANCTIONS FOR MISCONDUCT

21.4.1 All sanctions for misconduct are individual sanctions that apply for the entire match and are recorded on the score sheet.

21.4.2 Repetition of misconduct by the same team member in the same match is penalized progressively (each further offence results in a more severe sanction for that team member).

21.4.3 No prior sanction is required for an expulsion or disqualification resulting from offensive or aggressive behaviour.

21.5 MISCONDUCT BEFORE AND BETWEEN SETS

Any misconduct committed before or between sets is sanctioned according to Rule 21.3 and the sanctions apply to the following set.

21.6 SUMMARY OF MISCONDUCT AND THE CARDS USED

Warning: no sanction – Level 1: verbal warning
Level 2: symbol Yellow Card

Penalty: sanction – symbol Red Card

Expulsion: sanction – symbol Red + Yellow cards together

Disqualification: sanction – symbol Red + Yellow cards separately
