# Volleyball Rules

DIAGRAM 11: OFFICIAL HAND SIGNALS OF THE REFEREES (PART 2)

5. **Substitution**  
Make a circular motion with the forearms around each other.

7. **Expulsion**  
Show both the red and yellow cards together to indicate expulsion of a team member for the rest of the set.

8. **Disqualification**  
Show the red and yellow cards separately to indicate disqualification of a team member for the rest of the match.

9. **End of set (or match)**  
Cross the forearms in front of the chest, hands open.

10. **Ball not tossed or not released at the service hit**  
Raise the extended arm, palm facing upward.

11. **Delay in service**  
Raise eight fingers, spread.

12. **Blocking fault or screening**  
Raise both arms vertically, palms facing forward.

13. **Positional or rotation fault**  
Make a circular motion with the index finger.

14. **Ball "in"**  
Point the arm and fingers toward the floor.

15. **Ball "out"**  
Raise the forearms vertically, hands open, palms facing the body.

17. **Double contact**  
Raise two fingers, spread apart.
