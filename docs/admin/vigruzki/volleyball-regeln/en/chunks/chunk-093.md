# Volleyball Rules

**Dribbling**  
Dribbling means letting the ball bounce (usually as preparation for tossing and serving). Other preparatory actions can include, among others, moving the ball from hand to hand.

**Ball retrievers**  
These are persons whose duty is to maintain the flow of the game by rolling the ball to the server between rallies.

**Floor wipers**  
These are staff whose duty is to keep the floor clean and, when necessary, dry after each rally using small towels.

**Rally point**  
This is the system in which a point is scored when a rally is won.

**Interval**  
The time between sets. The change of courts in the fifth (deciding) set is not considered an interval.

**Re‑designation**  
This is the process by which a Libero who cannot continue playing, or is declared "unable to play" by the team, is replaced in the Libero role by another player (except the regular substitute) who is not on the court at the moment of re‑designation.

**Substitution (Libero replacement)**  
This is the action by which a regular player leaves the court and one of the two Liberos (if there are two) takes his/her place. It may also include Libero‑for‑Libero exchanges. The regular player may then replace one of the two Liberos. Between such replacements there must be a completed rally …
