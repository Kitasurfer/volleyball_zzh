# Volleyball Rules

DIAGRAM 11: OFFICIAL HAND SIGNALS OF THE REFEREES (PART 3)

16. **Caught ball**  
Raise the forearm slowly, palm facing upward.

18. **Four hits**  
Raise four fingers, spread.

19. **Net contact by a player / served ball touching the net and not crossing**  
Point to the corresponding side of the net with the appropriate hand.

20. **Reaching beyond the net**  
Place one hand over the net, palm facing downward.

21. **Attack‑hit fault**  
Make a downward movement with the forearm, hand open.  
This includes, for example:
- an illegal attack hit by a back‑row player or the Libero, or on the opponent’s service;  
- an illegal attack hit on a ball coming from an overhand finger pass by the Libero in his/her front zone or its extension.

22. **Penetration into the opponent’s court / faults related to the lower space or service position**  
Point to the centre line or the relevant line, indicating:
- penetration into the opponent’s court where the ball crosses through the lower space under the net; or
- the server touching the playing court (end line); or
- a player leaving his/her position at the moment of the service hit.

23. **Double fault and replay**  
Raise both thumbs vertically to indicate a double fault and that the rally is to be replayed.
