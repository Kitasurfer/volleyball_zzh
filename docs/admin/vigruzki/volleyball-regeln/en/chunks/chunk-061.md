18 INTERVALS AND CHANGE OF COURTS

18.1 INTERVALS

An interval is the time between sets. All intervals last three minutes.

During this time, the change of courts and the team line‑ups are recorded on the score sheet.

The interval between the second and third sets may be extended up to 10 minutes at the organizer’s request, subject to approval by the competent body.

18.2 CHANGE OF COURTS

18.2.1 After each set, the teams change courts, except before the deciding set.

18.2.2 In the deciding set, the teams change courts immediately once the leading team reaches 8 points, keeping the same player positions.
