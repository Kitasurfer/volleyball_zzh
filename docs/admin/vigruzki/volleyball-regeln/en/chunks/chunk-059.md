15.11.2 The first illegal request by a team in a match, which does not affect or delay the game, is rejected but must be recorded on the score sheet without further consequences.

15.11.3 Any further illegal request in the same match by the same team constitutes a delay.

A team’s illegal action that delays the resumption of the game is a delay and includes, among others:

16.1.1 delaying regular game interruptions;

16.1.2 prolonging interruptions after being instructed to resume play;

16.1.3 requesting an illegal substitution;

16.1.4 repeating an illegal request;

16.1.5 delaying the game by a team member.

16.2.1 'Delay warning' and 'delay penalty' are team sanctions.

16.2.1.1 Delay sanctions remain in force for the entire match.

16.2.1.2 All delay sanctions are recorded on the score sheet.
