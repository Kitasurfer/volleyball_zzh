# Volleyball Rules

… of the court.

When, in FIVB, world and official competitions, four line judges are used, they stand in the free zone 1 to 3 m from each corner of the court, on the imaginary extension of the line they control.

29.2 RESPONSIBILITIES

29.2.1 The line judges perform their duties using flags (40 x 40 cm) to signal:

29.2.1.1 whether the ball is "in" or "out" when the ball lands near their line(s);

29.2.1.2 touches of "out" balls by the receiving team;

29.2.1.3 the ball touching the antenna, and the served ball, the second and third team hit that cross the net outside the crossing space, etc.;

29.2.1.4 any player (other than the server) who leaves his/her position at the moment of the service hit;

29.2.1.5 foot faults by the server;

29.2.1.6 any contact with the top 80 cm of the antenna on their side of the court by a player while playing the ball or interfering with play;

29.2.1.7 the ball crossing the net outside the crossing space into the opponent’s court or touching the antenna on their side of the court.

29.2.2 At the request of the first referee, a line judge must repeat his/her signal.
