# Volleyball Rules

23.3.2 During the match, he/she is entitled to:

23.3.2.1 issue warnings to the teams;

23.3.2.2 sanction misconduct and delays;

23.3.2.3 decide on:

(a) serving faults and positional faults of the serving team, including net faults;

(b) faults in playing the ball;

(c) faults above the net and illegal contact of a player with the net, mainly (but not exclusively) on the attackers side;

(d) attack‑hit faults by the Libero and back‑row players;

(e) the completed attack hit by a player on a ball entirely above net height that comes from an overhand finger pass by the Libero in his/her front zone;

(f) the ball that completely crosses the lower space under the net;

(g) the completed block by a back‑row player or the attempted block by the Libero;

(h) the ball that crosses the net wholly or partly outside the crossing space to the opponents court or touches the antenna on his/her side of the court.
